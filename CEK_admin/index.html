About This Portal

Welcome to the Teachers' Portal, a dedicated platform for teachers to manage their profiles, view recent posts, and interact with students and alumni. As a teacher, you can log in or sign up to start using the portal.

After logging in, you'll be able to access various features including managing posts, verifying or deleting users, and more.
